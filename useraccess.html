NEXT User Access Features

NEXT offers a user account feature that provides personalized benefits to enhance your travel experience. By creating an account, you can save preferences, receive notifications, and access exclusive features.

Our user-friendly platform ensures that creating an account is quick and easy, allowing you to start enjoying the benefits immediately.

Account Benefits

Save Preferences

Store your preferred settings, such as language, accessibility options, and favorite stations, for a more personalized experience.

Receive Notifications

Get real-time updates on changes to your scheduled journeys, including platform alterations and service disruptions.

Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored suggestions for nearby facilities, services, and events based on your preferences and travel history.

Access Exclusive Features

Unlock exclusive features, such as advanced navigation tools and priority support, available only to registered users.

Track Journey History

Review your past journeys and access detailed information about your travel history, including routes, schedules, and expenses.

Seamless Integration

Enjoy a seamless experience as your user account integrates with other services, such as mobile apps and digital kiosks.
