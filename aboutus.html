Our History

NEXT was founded in 2020 with a vision to simplify navigation in railway stations. Our team of experts came together to create a solution that enhances the travel experience for everyone, ensuring accessibility and real-time information.

Our Motivation

At NEXT, we believe that every traveler deserves a seamless journey. Our motivation stems from the desire to empower individuals with the tools they need to navigate confidently, making travel easier and more enjoyable.
